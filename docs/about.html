About us

We are a dynamic online insurance broker that is transforming the very business model of insurance. We are the 1st online insurance platform in Singapore solely dedicated to serve all the insurance needs of small and medium business owners.

We hope to create an awesome experience that is fast, affordable and hassle-free.

How are we different from the traditional insurance brokers?

We are build differently. Our advanced technologically driven online insurance platform allow business owners to dynamically compare price and product features over an extensive range of commercial insurance products and to instantaneously purchase their desired insurance solutions.

We feature within our platform a comprehensive range of commercial insurance products. This way, business owners will enjoy savings without the inefficiencies associated with the traditional insurance business model.
